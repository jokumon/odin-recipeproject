Masala:

Ingredients:

  • 9 tbsp Besan
  • 3/4 tbsp Red chilli powder
  • Salt 3/4 tsps
  • 1/4 tsps tumeric powder
  • 1 tsps baking soda
  • 1 tbsp special masala

Steps:

  1. Mix besan, salt, red chilli powder, tumeric powder, special masala, baking soda
  2. Mix
  3. Slowly add water and whisk until a "muddy" consistency
  4. Add "condiments", these could be onions, spinach, potato, coriander
  5. heat oil on medium heat
  6. add a bit of batter in oil to test. If it stats sizzling, frying with bubbles, you're ready to fry pakoray
  7. Slowly add batter in "bits" with condiments. Do not over crowd
  8. take out and place on kitchen tissue when the colour seen in the above picture
