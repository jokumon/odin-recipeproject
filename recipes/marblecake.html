Marble Cake:

Ingredients:

  • 100g butter
  • 100 ml oil
  • Salt 1/2 teaspoons
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Vanilla Essence 2-3 drops
  • 150g sugar
  • Milk as required ( 1 cup)
  • Coco powder as required
  • Melted chocolate

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Beat butter, oil and sugar together in a bowl until creamy
  3. Add eggs, one by one to the butter and sugar. Beat.
  4. Add vanilla essence
  5. sift flour with baking powder and 1/2 tsp salt into the batter and fold with a spatula (circle then cross)
  6. Add milk as required
  7. Divide the batter into 2 bowls, in one bowl add melted chocolate and Coco powder to get a dark brown color
